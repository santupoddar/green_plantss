About Us

Cultivating Green Homes Across India with GreenNest

At GreenNest, our story began with a simple seed of an idea: to make the joy and well-being that plants bring accessible to every home in India. Founded in 2025 by TEAM-B. GreenNest sprouted from a shared passion for nature and a desire to bridge the gap between lush greenery and modern Indian living. Like many of you, we experienced firsthand the transformative power of bringing a little bit of the outdoors inside. The vibrant hues, the calming presence, the subtle air purification – plants enrich our lives in countless ways. However, we also recognized the challenges of finding healthy, diverse, and well-cared-for plants, especially with the convenience of online shopping that busy Indian lifestyles demand. That's where GreenNest took root. We envisioned a digital haven where plant enthusiasts, from seasoned green thumbs to curious beginners, could discover a curated collection of thriving plants sourced with care. We wanted to eliminate the hassle of traditional nurseries, offering a seamless online experience with detailed information, vibrant imagery, and the assurance of healthy plants delivered right to your doorstep, anywhere in India.
